Content

Home page

Welcome to Koran Music school where your desire to learn music will be satisfied in a fun and exciting way. Koran music school is a school that is aim at promoting music education. Music is part of every individual and can be a way we express our emotions. At Koran music school, we believe that everyone can learn music skills at their own pace and in their own comfort zone.

Images for the Home page

Courses

Koran music school provides a variety of courses for people in all age groups. Our courses can also be tailored to meet the busy schedules of our students. We offer in-person tutoring, a blend of online and in-person or online only depending on the nature of the course and the needs if the students. we have a piano beginners class for children and adults, an intermediate class and a piano master class. We also offer music notation, sight reading and sofa notation. Students are also free to specify the areas of study they prefer to engage in. This page will provide all the courses offered by Koran music school.

Contact Us

Koran music school is always available to provide our students and potential students with all the necessary information. We are available on various social media platforms and we can also be reached through email. This page will provide all the means of contact for koran music school.
